Nepal is a beautiful himalayan country sandwiched between India and China. 
It is a landlocked country. 
It has an area of 1,47,181 sq.km.

The capital city of Nepal is Kathmandu.
Politically, Nepal is divided into 7 provinces.

Nepal is rich in history, culture and art. There are many temples and stupas in Nepal.

The highest mountain of the world, Mt.Everest, lies in Nepal.There are also many magnificient mountains, rivers, lakes and valleys in Nepal.
