Ethan and Amanda sat on the couch in her apartment watching a movie. 
After a bit, Amanda volunteered to make a snack.
It wasn’t long before Ethan decided to investigate.
Amanda stood casually beside the microwave listening to the sound kernels popping.
She didn’t seem to notice the burnt smell in the air at all, but when Ethan walked in, she gave him an apologetic smile. 

“I always burn the first bag.” She confessed.

In that moment, Ethan knew she was the one.
